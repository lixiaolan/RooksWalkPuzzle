About Us

Seventh Harmonic was started by two graduate students in math at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and later joined by one more. We can be found in various coffee shops in the Madison area and we are always down for chatting with you. Neither of us knew anything about Android or small business ownership when we started. Today, we have both a product and a business! Our vision for Seventh Harmonic is still evolving. Will we remain a game company or will we git branched out? Will we grow in numbers or will we stay a small operation? Will we hold fast to our ideals and ambitions or sell out and go work for Google? Jkz, Google, we love you! Time alone will reveal all.

Lalit Jain
